## App Icon Concept

A single glowing orb on a deep dark background (#050508). The orb has three visual layers:
- **Core:** Warm white (#f0e6d3) solid center
- **Inner glow:** Radial gradient emanating from the center
- **Outer halo:** Soft ambient ring with warm cream (#e8d5c4) tones

The icon should feel like looking at a distant star through a telescope — calm, luminous, and singular. No text, no other elements. Just the orb.

**Design references:**
- Apple Watch Breathe app, but more refined
- Planetarium star with soft glow
- Single candle flame in a dark room

## App Description

**Short Description (170 chars):**
> Open. Breathe. Done. The meditation app that gets out of your way.

**Full Description:**

Muse is the meditation app for people who hate meditation apps.

No narrations. No playlists. No backgrounds of rainforests. No seven-day courses. Just open it, set a duration, and breathe.

The orb expands and contracts on your screen. You breathe in as it grows. You breathe out as it shrinks. Haptic feedback marks each transition. When your time is up, it fades gently — and you get on with your day.

**How it works:**
- Tap the orb to begin
- Breathe in as it expands (4 seconds)
- Hold at peak (2 seconds)
- Breathe out as it contracts (4 seconds)
- Hold at bottom (2 seconds)
- Repeat until your session ends

**Sessions:**
- Choose any duration from 1 to 60 minutes
- Gentle haptic cues on each phase transition
- Session complete haptic when you're done
- Optional completion tone

**Pricing:**
- **Free:** 5 sessions per day, up to 10 minutes each
- **Practice ($4.99/mo):** Unlimited sessions, up to 45 minutes, session history
- **Master ($9.99/mo):** Everything in Practice, plus custom breathing patterns, guided breath holds, advanced haptic patterns, and Apple Watch companion

**Why Muse:**
- 100% private. No account required.
- No data collection. No subscriptions required for core use.
- Sessions as short as 1 minute — or as long as 60.
- Works offline. No internet required.
- Beautiful, distraction-free design that stays out of your way.
