## Description

MuseMac transforms your Mac into a deeply personal music experience. A dark, immersive player built for listeners who care about more than just playback — they care about presence.

**Core Features:**
- **AI Playlist Generator** — Describe your mood, get a curated playlist in seconds. Focus, unwind, energize, or dream — MuseMac understands.
- **Live Listening Sessions** — Share a real-time listening room with friends. See what they're playing, react, and stay in sync.
- **Collaborative Playlists** — Build playlists together. Invite friends, contribute tracks, watch the playlist evolve.
- **Listening Insights** — Understand your music taste with weekly activity charts, genre breakdowns, and top artist stats.
- **Now Playing** — A gorgeous, waveform-driven player with progress scrubbing, volume control, and seamless queue management.
- **Menu Bar Mini Player** — Control playback without leaving your workflow.

**Design Philosophy:**
Dark vinyl-black surfaces. Purple-to-pink gradient accents. Typography that breathes. An interface that steps back so the music steps forward.
